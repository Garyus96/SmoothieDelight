What is Smoothie Delight?

Smoothie Delight is a brand new line of smoothies, which allows you to design your own unique smoothie and then add it to your daily cathering.

There are 4 following steps:

  • Log in to your account
  • Enter ingredients for your smoothie
  • When you are all set, click the "add to recipes" button
  • All done! Your freshly made smoothie has been saved to your recipe list

We care about the highest quality of our smoothies

Our products are made with specially selected fresh fruits and vegetables, providing you the best possible experience.
